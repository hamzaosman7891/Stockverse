Features

Buy & Sell Stocks

Buy and sell your favorite stocks with your default cash balance of $100,000, and view them in your portfolio summary. You can add or withdraw funds.

Portfolio Distribution

View the percentage of the stocks owned in your portfolio through a pie chart. The chart will contain a different colour for each stock owned and its total value.

Data

Obtain infomration on potential stocks that you are intrested on the quote page. Enter a stock symbol and obtain information on real time prices.

About


StockVerse

Stockverse is a portfolio managment application, that allows users to obtain information on stocks. Users can enter the symbol of any stock and obtain information on real time prices and market cap. Users will be able to buy and sell shares of any stocks. When selling an owned stock users will obtain infomration on estimated proceeds and estimated profit and loss. On the homepage users will be able to view their portfolio summary as a table and on the history page users will be able to view all their transactions.

Create your own portfolio and view your distribution chart.
